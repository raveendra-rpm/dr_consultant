Refund & Cancellation

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Appointment Cancellation by Patient

We understand that unexpected events occur. If you need to cancel your appointment, please note our policy:

  • Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled consultation time are eligible for a 100% refund.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled time are non-refundable.
  • No-shows: If you fail to join the online consultation at the scheduled time without prior notice, the fee will be forfeited and no refund will be provided.

2. Rescheduling Policy

  • You may reschedule your appointment for free up to 12 hours before the scheduled time.
  • Rescheduling requests made less than 12 hours prior may be treated as a cancellation, subject to the doctor's discretion.

3. Cancellation by the Doctor/Clinic

In rare circumstances, Dr. Divya Singh may need to cancel or reschedule an appointment due to medical emergencies or unforeseen technical issues. In such cases:

  • We will notify you as soon as possible.
  • You will be offered a priority rescheduled slot.
  • If the new time does not work for you, a full 100% refund will be initiated immediately.

4. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be credited back to your original mode of payment (Credit Card, UPI, Net Banking, etc.) within 5 to 7 business days depending on your bank's processing times.
