2. Nature of Online Consultation

Our teleconsultation service connects patients with Dr. Divya Singh for non-emergency medical advice. Please note that online consultations have inherent limitations compared to physical examinations. The advice provided is based on the medical history and information you provide.

IMPORTANT: In case of a medical emergency (e.g., severe pain, heavy bleeding, loss of consciousness), please visit the nearest hospital or emergency room immediately. Do not rely on online consultations for urgent care.
