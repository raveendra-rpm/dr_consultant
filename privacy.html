Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Dr. Divya Singh's Online Consultation Platform. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our patients' personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online medical consultation services.

2. Information We Collect

During your interaction with our online consultation platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, age, gender, contact number, email address, and residential address.
  • Medical Information: Past medical history, current symptoms, prescriptions, medical reports, and other health-related data necessary for accurate diagnosis.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details necessary for processing consultation fees (processed securely via third-party payment gateways; we do not store full credit card details).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing and managing online medical consultations and generating e-prescriptions.
  • Communicating with you regarding appointment confirmations, rescheduling, and follow-ups.
  • Processing payments securely.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory medical record-keeping obligations in India.

4. Data Protection & Confidentiality

We adhere to strict medical confidentiality protocols. All video consultations are end-to-end encrypted, and your medical records are stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical data to third parties.
